Property Prices in European Countries

Country Notes
Bulgaria Considered one of the cheapest countries in Europe to buy property, especially in rural areas and small towns.
Romania Offers affordable properties, especially outside the capital Bucharest.
Hungary Secondary cities like Debrecen and Szeged offer reasonably priced properties.
Bosnia and Herzegovina Property prices are low compared to other European countries.
Albania Property prices remain relatively low, especially in coastal cities.
Serbia Belgrade and other cities offer affordable property options.
Poland While Warsaw can be expensive, other cities like Krakow and Łódź offer affordable options.
Latvia Properties in Riga and other cities are reasonably priced.
Slovakia Apart from Bratislava, you can find reasonably priced properties.
Croatia Some areas outside the well-known tourist destinations offer good property prices.
